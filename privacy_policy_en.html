Sea Hunter — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Developer: APRYCO

Overview

Sea Hunter is a fully offline mobile game. We respect our users' privacy and do not collect any personal data.

Data Collection

This application does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • There is no user account or login system.
  • No personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location is requested or collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are included.

Data Storage

All game data (progress, achievements, settings) is stored locally on the user's device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Since the application does not collect any personal information from any user, it is safe for all age groups, including children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page.
